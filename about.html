Cumberland Valley Technology Student Association

What is TSA?

TSA is a national, non-profit organization of high school and middle school student members who are engaged in STEM. Since TSA was chartered in 1978, over 5,000,000 members have participated through competitions, intracurricular activities, leadership opportunities, and community service.

Our Chapter Officers

Our officers help run and monitor our TSA chapter. They provide assistance to our fellow members and help us improve as a whole. Each year, we elect new members to be a part of the team. There are officers in our high school as well as the two middle schools.
